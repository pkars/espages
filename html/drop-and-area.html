Monster Drop

  • Junk includes Potions, Keys, TP/ID Scrolls and Quivers.
  • Equipment includes Weapons, Armor and Ancient Coupons.
  • Good includes Rings, Amulets, Charms, Jewels, Gems, Runes, Ancient Scrolls, Dragon Stones, Ancient Decipherers and Socket Donuts.
  • 300% Chance for ~ items means the monster have 3 times higher chance for those items than non-boss monsters.

    • Quarterback, Defensive End, Defensive Tackle, Cornerback, Safety

  • The chance of no drop looks greater than the old value listed in FAQ, but the actual value isn't changed. (Junk and Gold drop contained another no drop chance in themselves in the older version, and FAQ didn't reflected that.)

Champions

  • Each champion always drops one random drop and one potion. (The random item can be a portion, too.)

  • 300% Chance for Unique/Set/Rare items.
  • The dropped equipment is always magic or better.
  • Gold drop is 6 times better than the non-boss monster's gold drop in LoD.
