Eastern Sun stuff

Eastern Sun is a mod for Diablo II. It was originally created by Lothar and Perfect Cell in early 2000's, and later updated by tsuru from late 2004/early 2005 onwards. The last update by tsuru is Eastern Sun version R6D (dated 20 Mar 2008), although there are a couple of more recent community patches floating around.

These pages contain information from the now-defunct Eastern Sun wiki. The original pages were obtained via Wayback Machine. (A snapshot from 20 Nov 2020.) Then the relevant parts of the pages were converted to markdown using Pandoc, cleaned up (i.e. fixed formatting where needed and removed the wiki-related code), and then converted back to html. The source files for these pages are available in my GitHub repository.
