Prayer †

Required Level
1
Prerequisites
None
Difference from the LoD version
Improved heal amount.
Pros
With bonuses from Synergies, Prayer can heal large amounts of health over time for little cost. Synergies to Prayer are not transferred to Cleansing and Meditation.
Cons
Cleansing and Meditation heal the same amount of life as Prayer heals without consuming Mana, assuming no Synergies to Prayer.
