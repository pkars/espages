Required Level
30
Prerequisites
Jab (1), Pyre Javelin (6), Plague Javelin (12), Frost Javelin (24)
Difference from the LoD version
The number of bolts increases slowly after slvl 30. Can be used with any throwing weapons.
Pros
Numerous lightning bolts are released every time the missile hits a monster. With a good amount of pierce, very effective against packed monsters.
Cons
Ineffective against a single target.
